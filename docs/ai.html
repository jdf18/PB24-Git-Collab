How AI contributes:

AI can contribute a lot to society, in particular its ability to learn to find patterns in data which we wouldn't otherwise be able to see. This allows for AI to do things such as classifying and detecting cancers better than we would normally be able to.

Rather than having people try to work out what a cancer is, how it will act and what treatments to try, we can potentially delegate the task to AI. It could, after some more development, be able to tell us this information quicker and maybe even more accurately than humans can.

AI can also be used for things such as facial recognition or number plate recognition. This can allow for the easier detection of criminals, helping make the roads and streets safer for everyone.
